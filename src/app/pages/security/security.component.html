Security Best Practices

How you can help keep your account secure

1

Enable 2FA

Always use two-factor authentication for an extra layer of security beyond your password.

2

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for your eStokvel account. Consider using a password manager.

3

Regular Updates

Keep your devices and browsers updated with the latest security patches.

4

Watch for Phishing

Be cautious of suspicious emails or messages asking for your login credentials.

5

Monitor Activity

Regularly review your account activity and report any unauthorized transactions immediately.

6

Secure Devices

Use device passwords, biometric locks, and avoid accessing your account on public computers.
