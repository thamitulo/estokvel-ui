1. Introduction

info

Last Updated: 1 January 2024

eStokvel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is collected and used properly, lawfully, and transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) and other applicable South African data protection laws. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information.

warning
Important: By using eStokvel, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined herein.

2. Definitions

menu_book

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

Personal Information

Information relating to an identifiable, living, natural person, and where applicable, an identifiable, existing juristic person.

Processing

Any operation or activity concerning personal information, including collection, storage, use, modification, or destruction.

Data Subject

The person to whom personal information relates (i.e., you, our user).

Responsible Party

The party who determines the purpose of and means for processing personal information (i.e., eStokvel).

Operator

A person who processes personal information for a responsible party in terms of a contract or mandate.

POPIA

Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013, South Africa's data protection law.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

share

We may share your personal information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Within Your Stokvel Group

  • Basic profile information (name, contact details) with other stokvel members
  • Contribution records and payment status within the group
  • Meeting attendance and participation information

5.2 With Service Providers (Operators)

  • Payment Processors: Banks and financial institutions for transaction processing
  • Cloud Services: Secure data storage providers with POPIA compliance
  • Communication Services: SMS and email service providers
  • Technical Support: IT maintenance and security services

All operators are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5.3 LegalComponent Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • To regulatory authorities (FSCA, SARS, etc.)
  • To law enforcement agencies with proper legal authority
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

warning
Cross-Border Transfers: We primarily store and process your information within South Africa. Any international transfers will only occur with adequate protection and in compliance with POPIA.
