What is POPIA?

The Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) is South Africa's primary data privacy legislation. It regulates how organisations collect, process, store, share and dispose of personal information belonging to individuals (data subjects).

POPIA came into full effect on 1 July 2021 and applies to all organisations that process personal information of South African residents.
