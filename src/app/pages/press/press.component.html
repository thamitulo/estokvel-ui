eStokvel turns stokvels into business entities

eStokvel, a South African fintech startup, is revolutionizing traditional stokvels by transforming them into formal business entities through its digital platform.

The platform enables stokvel groups to manage their finances transparently, access banking services, and operate with legal recognition, bridging the gap between informal savings groups and formal financial systems.

Key Highlights:

  • Digital transformation of traditional stokvels
  • Legal entity status for stokvel groups
  • Transparent financial management tools
  • Integration with formal banking systems
  • Focus on financial inclusion and empowerment
