Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about working with us

What's the typical interview timeline?

Our process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application to offer. We move quickly because we respect your time and know great talent gets snapped up fast.

 Do you offer remote work?

Yes! We're a hybrid-first company. Most roles offer flexibility to work remotely or from our offices in major South African cities.

 What's the career growth like?

We provide clear career paths, regular performance reviews, and support for both technical and leadership growth tracks.

 Do you sponsor work visas?

Yes, we sponsor visas for exceptional candidates. We're building a diverse team with talent from across Africa and beyond.

 What's the company culture like?

We're driven by our mission, collaborative by nature, and believe in work-life harmony. We celebrate wins together and support each other through challenges.

 Do you have internship programs?

Yes! We offer paid internships in engineering, design, and marketing. Check our open positions for current internship opportunities.

Ready to Build the Future with Us?

If you don't see a perfect match but believe you'd be a great addition to our team, we'd still love to hear from you!
