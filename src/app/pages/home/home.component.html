What is a Stokvel?

Community.
Savings.

A stokvel is a savings or investment society where members regularly contribute agreed amounts and receive lump sum payouts.

With eStokvel, choose between private (invite-only) or public (anyone can join) stokvels. Minimum of 3 members required to start.
