What is a Stokvel?

Community. Savings. Growth.

A stokvel is a savings or investment society where members regularly contribute agreed amounts and receive lump sum payouts in rotation.

With eStokvel, choose between private (invite-only) or public (anyone can join) stokvels. Minimum of 3 members required to start.
